How to

One thing that I'm going to teach the class (that only works on Windows computer) is that when you copy something but accidentally copied something else and you already deleted that pages where you found the thing you wanted, you can press the Windows key and the v key at the same time to see your clipboard history where it displays several items that you've copied. It only works if you turn on your clipboard history which is in settings and I found this especially useful for pictures and photos because I can just restore the photo I want by a click.
